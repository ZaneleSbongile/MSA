Here is a YouTube channel to help you. My Story Animated is a digital platform that provides teenagers and adults the most interesting and life changing stories with the world. MSA, also known as My Story Animated, is an anime storytime channel owned by the company Zvoid which is based on fictional stories from the viewers.It is a YouTube channel that actually animates real life stories sent to them by teenagers, It has over 21.9M subscribers. Zvoid is the creator and producer of “My Story Animated”, one of the most recognizable animated shows in the world, which has become a global sensation among young adults worldwide.With an average of 60+ million monthly unique viewers, 10+ million of whom watch daily, people look to My Story Animated not only as a form of entertainment but also as a source of inspiration. “My Story Animated” offers storytelling at its finest, with stories that aim to inspire the next generation to become strong-willed adults who stand up for their beliefs.We tell life stories of people who face hardships in their lives but overcome them through perseverance, positivity, and intelligence. The idea of the channel is to tell outlandish stories that occur to teenage characters. They involve school, family secrets, and other real world topics, however they usually have an unusual twist to them
